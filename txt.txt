The rules for playing Memory Game:
1. All the cards are mixed up.
2. They are visible only for one second, then turned face down.
3. You should turn over any two cards.
4. If this two cards match, they stay visible.
5. If they don't match, they are turned back over.
6. You should remember what was on each card and where it was.
7. The game is over when all the cards have been matched.

Paw Patrol is an animated tv series showing the adventures of a boy named Ryder who is the leader of a pack of search and rescue dogs known as the Paw Patrol. Each dog has a specific set of skills, so they are protecting the neighbourhood as a firefighter or a police officer. They are equipped with special vehicles and backpacks that contain tools that relate to their jobs.
